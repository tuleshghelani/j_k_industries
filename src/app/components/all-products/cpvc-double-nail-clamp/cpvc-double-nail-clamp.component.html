Installation Guide

1

Measure and Mark

Determine optimal placement points along your CPVC pipe run, typically every 80-100cm for horizontal installations. Mark wall positions ensuring proper alignment.

2

Position the Clamp

Place the CPVC double nail clamp against the wall at your marked position. Ensure the clamp size matches your pipe diameter and the opening faces the direction for easy pipe insertion.

3

Secure First Nail

Insert the first nail through the primary mounting hole. Using a hammer or power tool, drive the nail until it's approximately 80% secured, allowing for minor adjustments.

4

Insert Second Nail

Place the second nail through the secondary mounting hole and drive it completely. This dual-nail system provides the superior holding power characteristic of our CPVC double nail clamps.

5

Finalize Installation

Complete driving the first nail fully. Insert the CPVC pipe into the clamp and ensure it sits properly. For hot water applications, allow slight movement for thermal expansion.
