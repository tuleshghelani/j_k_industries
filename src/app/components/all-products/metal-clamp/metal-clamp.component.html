Installation Best Practices for Metal Clamps

Proper installation of metal clamps is essential for optimal performance and longevity. Our metal pipe clamps should be installed at intervals of 60-100cm for horizontal pipe runs and at every change of direction. For vertical installations, pipe clamps should be spaced appropriately to support the pipe weight and prevent sagging.

Installation Tools and Requirements

Installing our metal clamps requires basic tools including a power drill or hammer, measuring tape, pencil for marking, and appropriate fasteners. For hard surfaces like concrete or brick, pre-drilling pilot holes ensures accurate placement.
