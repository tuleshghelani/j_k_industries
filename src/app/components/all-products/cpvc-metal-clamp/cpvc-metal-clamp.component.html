Frequently Asked Questions

What makes CPVC powder coated metal clamps better than standard plastic clips?

CPVC powder coated metal clamps combine the strength of metal with specialized CRC / MS that protects CPVC pipes. Unlike standard plastic clips, they provide superior load-bearing capacity, better chemical resistance, higher temperature tolerance (up to 93°C), and longer service life.

Are your CPVC powder coated clamps suitable for hot water systems?

Yes, our CPVC powder coated metal clamps are specifically designed for hot water systems. They can withstand continuous temperatures up to 93°C (200°F), making them ideal for hot water distribution lines in residential, commercial, and industrial applications. The specialized CRC / MS accommodates thermal expansion while maintaining secure support of the piping system.
