Frequently Asked Questions

What makes stainless steel clamps better than regular steel clamps?

Stainless steel clamps offer superior corrosion resistance, longer service life, and better performance in extreme environments. Unlike regular steel clamps, SS clamps don't rust when exposed to moisture or chemicals, making them ideal for critical applications where reliability is essential. They also maintain their structural integrity at higher temperatures.

Are your SS clamps suitable for outdoor applications?

Yes, our stainless steel clamps are perfect for outdoor applications. They resist corrosion from rain, humidity, UV exposure, and environmental pollutants. The SS316 grade is particularly suitable for coastal environments where salt spray is present.

Do you offer custom sizes for stainless steel clamps?

Yes, we manufacture custom-sized stainless steel clamps according to specific requirements. Our engineering team can design and produce clamps for non-standard diameters, unique load requirements, or special applications. Please contact our sales team with your specifications for a custom quote.

What grade of stainless steel do you use for your clamps?

We primarily use SS304 (austenitic stainless steel) for standard applications and SS316 for marine and highly corrosive environments. Both grades offer excellent corrosion resistance and mechanical properties. We can also source and manufacture using other specialized grades upon request.
