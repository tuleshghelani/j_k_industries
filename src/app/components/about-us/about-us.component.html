About JK Industries

Our Story in Clamp Manufacturing

Founded in 2010, JK Industries has established itself as a premier manufacturer of high-quality industrial clamps, serving clients across various sectors with precision-engineered clamping solutions.

Based in Rajkot, Gujarat, our state-of-the-art facility specializes in manufacturing premium pipe clamps, nico clamps, and UPVC/CPVC metal clamps that meet the highest industry standards for durability and performance.

Our commitment to quality and innovation has positioned us as a trusted partner for industries requiring reliable clamping solutions for their critical applications.
