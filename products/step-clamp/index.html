How to Install Step Clamps | Step Metal Clamps | Step Pipe Clamps Installation Guide

Follow these steps for proper installation of step clamps, metal clamps, step pipe clamps, and step metal clamps

1

Preparation

Mark the position ensuring proper pipe alignment and spacing (recommended every 80-100cm for horizontal runs). Ensure the mounting surface is clean and free from debris.

2

Pre-drilling

For hard surfaces like concrete or brick, pre-drill pilot holes using a 3mm drill bit to ensure accurate placement and prevent surface cracking.

3

Positioning

Place the step clamp against the mounting surface, ensuring proper alignment with the marked position. Verify the clamp size matches your pipe diameter requirements.

4

Fastening

Secure the step clamp using appropriate fasteners (screws or bolts) until the clamp is firmly attached to the mounting surface. Ensure the clamp is flush and secure.

5

Verification

Check that the pipe sits securely in the appropriate step of the clamp without being compressed, ensuring proper movement allowance for thermal expansion. Verify the installation is stable.
