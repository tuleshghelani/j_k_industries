How to Install UPVC Double Nail Clamps | Nail Clamp Installation Guide

Follow these steps for proper installation of UPVC double nail clamps, metal clamps, and dual fastening clamps

1

Preparation

Mark the position ensuring proper pipe alignment and spacing (recommended every 80-100cm for horizontal runs)

2

Pre-drilling

For hard surfaces, pre-drill pilot holes using a 3mm drill bit to prevent splitting and ensure accurate nail placement

3

Positioning

Place the clamp against the mounting surface, ensuring both nail guides are properly aligned with the marked positions

4

Fastening

Insert both hardened steel nails and drive them simultaneously using a hammer or power tool until flush with the clamp surface

5

Verification

Check that the pipe sits securely in the clamp without being compressed, ensuring proper movement allowance for thermal expansion

How do I install UPVC double nail clamps correctly?
