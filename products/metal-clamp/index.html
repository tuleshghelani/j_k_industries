How to Install Metal Clamps - Installation Guide

Follow these steps for proper installation of metal clamps and pipe clamps

1

Preparation

Mark the position ensuring proper pipe alignment and spacing. For metal clamps, recommended spacing is every 60-100cm for horizontal runs and at each change of direction.

2

Pre-drilling

For hard surfaces like concrete or brick, pre-drill pilot holes using a 3-4mm drill bit to ensure accurate placement and prevent surface cracking when installing metal clamps.

3

Positioning

Place the metal clamp against the mounting surface, ensuring the clamp saddle is properly aligned with the intended pipe position. Verify the clamp size matches the pipe diameter.

4

Fastening

Secure the metal clamp using appropriate fasteners (screws, nails, or anchors) until the clamp is firmly attached. For double nail clamps, use a hammer to drive the integrated nails.

5

Verification

Check that the pipe sits securely in the metal clamp without being compressed. Ensure proper movement allowance for thermal expansion, especially for hot water CPVC installations.
