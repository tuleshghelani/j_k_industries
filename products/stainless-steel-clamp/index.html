How to Install Metal Clamps | SS Pipe Clamp Installation Guide

Follow these steps for proper installation of stainless steel clamps, SS metal clamps, and SS pipe clips

1

Preparation

Mark the position ensuring proper pipe alignment and spacing. For stainless steel metal clamps, recommended spacing is every 60-80cm for horizontal runs.

2

Pre-drilling

For hard surfaces like concrete or brick, pre-drill pilot holes using a 4mm drill bit to ensure accurate mounting of your SS metal clamp.

3

Positioning

Place the stainless steel clamp against the mounting surface, ensuring the clamp saddle is properly aligned with the pipe position.

4

Fastening

Insert appropriate screws or anchors and drive them using a screwdriver or power tool until the SS pipe clamp is securely mounted.

5

Verification

Check that the pipe sits securely in the metal clamp without being compressed. Ensure proper movement allowance for thermal expansion.

The 0.5mm thickness metal clamps are suitable for lighter applications and smaller pipe sizes, offering cost-effectiveness while maintaining durability.
