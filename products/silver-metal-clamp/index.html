Frequently Asked Questions

What is the advantage of Silver Metal Clamps over GI Clamps?

Silver Metal Clamps offer a superior aesthetic finish and smoother surface compared to standard GI (Galvanized Iron) clamps. While both offer corrosion resistance, silver clamps are specifically designed for applications where looks matter, providing a brighter, cleaner appearance that resists dulling over time.

Can these clamps be used for outdoor plumbing?

Yes, our Silver Metal Clamps are treated for weather resistance. However, for extreme coastal or highly corrosive industrial environments, we recommend our Stainless Steel (SS) range. For general outdoor use in standard conditions, silver clamps perform excellently.

Do you offer custom sizes for special projects?

Absolutely. As a direct manufacturer, JK Industries can produce "Edler" brand silver metal clamps in custom sizes, widths, and thicknesses to match your specific project requirements. Minimum order quantities may apply for custom dies.

How do I maintain the shine of these clamps?

Our silver clamps are maintenance-free for the most part. In dusty environments, a simple wipe with a dry or slightly damp cloth is enough to restore their original shine. Avoid using harsh acidic cleaners.

Is the "Edler" brand your own manufacturing?

Yes, "Edler" is the premium brand of JK Industries. All Edler clamps are manufactured in our state-of-the-art facility in Rajkot, Gujarat, ensuring direct-from-factory pricing and quality control.
