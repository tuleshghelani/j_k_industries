How to Install CPVC Double Nail Clamps | Nail Clamp Installation Guide

Follow these steps for proper installation of CPVC double nail clamps, metal clamps, and dual fastening clamps in hot water systems

1

Preparation

Mark the position ensuring proper CPVC pipe alignment and spacing (recommended every 80-100cm for horizontal runs, 60-80cm for hot water lines)

2

Pre-drilling

For hard surfaces like concrete or brick, pre-drill pilot holes using a 3mm drill bit to ensure accurate nail placement and prevent surface cracking

3

Positioning

Place the CPVC double nail clamp against the mounting surface, ensuring both nail guides are properly aligned with the marked positions

4

Fastening

Insert both heat-treated ceramic coated nails and drive them simultaneously using a hammer or power tool until flush with the clamp surface

5

Verification

Check that the CPVC pipe sits securely in the clamp without being compressed, ensuring proper movement allowance for thermal expansion in hot water applications
